About the DAF Online App

The Delayed Audio Feedback (DAF) Online App is an easy-to-use, web-based tool designed to assist users in improving speech fluency and managing speech-related challenges. With no need for downloads or installations, users can access the DAF system directly from their browser on desktops, tablets, or smartphones.
